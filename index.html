Short bio. (Brief)

Hanokh (Henry) Aloni,

Although his job title is "Enterprise solutions department manager", he is actually 1 part executive and 1 part software engineer. Hanokh is an experienced, hands-on software engineer. He has expertise in designing, implementing and supporting distributed software, service-oriented systems, and even smart web clients. Hanokh also manages and teaches how to manage software projects. He also hosts some software/project related meetups and events : the local Global Game Jam site is turning 9 years old, a quarterly architectural kata as well as training lectures and tech meetups. Hanokh has the ability to see and plan the big picture as well as do the hands-on dirty work (coding, compiling, testing, and deploying) in various languages and environment. A true polyglot - a jack of all trades.

Hanokh has a bachelor of technology in the field of software engineering. He lives and works in an office located in Be'er Sheva, Israel.
